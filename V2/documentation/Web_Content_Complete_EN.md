### History Section
**Title:**
Our Story

**Content:**
Valderrama International School was founded in 2010 with a clear mission: to provide world-class bilingual education to students in Cartagena and surrounding areas. Located in the heart of this historic city, we have grown to become a recognized institution for academic excellence, an inclusive environment, and commitment to the integral development of our students.

Our name honors Spanish military strategist Pedro Navarro, who built innovative fortifications. Similarly, we build strong foundations in our students so they can face 21st-century challenges.

### Admissions Process Section
**Title:**
Admissions Process

**Description:**
Our admissions process is designed to be fair, transparent, and comprehensive.

**Steps:**

1. **Initial Consultation**
   - Contact our admissions department
   - Receive detailed information about programs and options

2. **Campus Tour**
   - Explore our campus
   - Interact with students and teachers
   - Ask questions

3. **Application Submission**
   - Complete the online form
   - Provide required documents
   - Pay processing fee (non-refundable)

4. **Interview and Evaluation**
   - Family interview with admissions committee
   - Student academic evaluation (varies by grade)
   - Language assessment (if applicable)

5. **Decision**
   - Receive notification within 2-3 weeks
   - Three possible outcomes: Admitted, Waitlist, Not Admitted

6. **Confirmation and Enrollment**
   - Sign admissions contract
   - Pay tuition and enrollment deposit
   - Receive information for start of school

---

### Required Documents Section
**Title:**
Required Documents

**For all applicants:**
- National ID or passport
- Birth certificate
- Vaccination record copy
- Most recent school reports or grade transcripts
- Academic recommendations (2)

**Grade-specific requirements:**
- **Kindergarten:** Developmental evaluation
- **Grades 1-5:** Reading and math assessment
- **Grades 6-8:** English, math, and reasoning tests
- **Grades 9-12:** Advanced assessments + complete academic transcript

---

### Scholarships and Financial Aid Section
**Title:**
Scholarships and Financial Aid

**Description:**
Valderrama offers academic and need-based scholarships for outstanding students.

**Scholarship Types:**

1. **Academic Scholarships**
   - Up to 50% of tuition
   - Based on academic performance
   - Renewable annually

2. **Athletic Scholarships**
   - Up to 40% of tuition
   - For outstanding athletes
   - Requires competitive commitment

3. **Arts Scholarships**
   - Up to 35% of tuition
   - For exceptional artists
   - Music, theater, or visual arts

4. **Need-Based Scholarships**
   - Up to 60% of tuition
   - Based on financial analysis
   - Requires income documentation

**Scholarship Application Process:**
- Complete scholarship application form
- Submit financial documentation
- Participate in evaluation or audition
- Decision provided with admissions notification

### Frequently Asked Questions Section
**Title:**
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: When is the admissions period?**
A: We accept applications throughout the year. The main period is October through March.

**Q: What is the student-teacher ratio?**
A: On average 12-15 students per teacher, varying by grade level.

**Q: Is uniform mandatory?**
A: Yes, uniform is required at all levels. Available at authorized stores.

**Q: Do you offer transportation?**
A: No. We recommend shared transportation between families or private services.

**Q: What if I don't speak English?**
A: Our bilingual program begins in Kindergarten. Language support is available.
